Bengali Chicken Curry

This is a spicy, delicious and soupy chicken curry with potatoes flavoured with aromatic spices. It is a quick and easy special recipe best eaten with piping hot rice and a squeeze of lime. Try it out and you will be satisfied.

Ingredients

  • Mustard oil
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 4 Green Cardamom pods
  • 2-inch cinnamon sticks
  • 2 Star anise
  • 4-6 Cloves
  • 1 tablespoon Garlic(chopped)
  • 1/2 Cup onion(chopped)
  • 1/2 Cup Tomato(chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger(chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin Seeds powder
  • Salt(as required)
  • Black pepper powder
  • 2 cups Water
  • Coriander Leaves

Recipe Methods

  • Marinating the Chicken 500gms Chicken(curry cut) add 1 teaspoon tumeric powder mix with salt as required.
  • For the potatoes do the same as for Chicken
  • Fly the marinated Chicken with mustard oil for 5-6minutes and do the same for the potatoes but separately later transfer the fried chicken and potatoes into their bowls respectively.
  • Add mustard a tablespoon of mustard oil into and in 2 Bay leaves,4 GreenCardamon pods, 2 cinamon sticks, 2 Star anise, 4-6 cloves, 1 tablespoon Garlic, 1/2 Cup onion, 1/2 Cup Tomato, 1 tbsp Ginger, 1 teaspoon Red Chilli, 1 Cumin seed powder, salt as required and some water.
  • Add the fried potatoes, also add 2 cups of water and wait until the water reach the boiling point then add the fried chicken and mix
  • Wait for to boil for at least 20-30minutes and after that the chicken curry is ready, add the Coriander leaves
