Special Chicken Gravy Recipe

This is a spicy, delicious and soupy chicken Gravy flavoured with aromatic spices. It is a quick and easy special recipe best eaten with Chapati or rice. Try it out and you will be satisfied.

Ingredients

  • Chicken, curry-cut pieces with bones-500gms
  • Onions(chopped- 2 medium)
  • Ginger Garlic(2 tsp)
  • Cashew nuts-10
  • Whole red chillies(5-6)
  • Red ripe tomatoes(2 medium)
  • Tumeric powder(1/2 tsp)
  • Red chilli powder(1 tsp)
  • Garam masala powder(1/2 tsp)
  • Oil (4 tbsp)
  • Salt (1 tsp)
  • Kasuri Methi (fenugreek leaves 1 tsp roasted and crushed)

Recipe Methods

  • Chop the onions and quarter the 2 red tomatoes
  • Dry roast the cashew nuts and the red chillies in a pan on low heat for 2-3minutes. Set aside to cool. Coarse grind first and then add the quartered tomatoes.Continue to blend it into a smooth paste.
  • Heat oil in a pan and add the chopped onions, fry on medium heat for 5-6minutes till translucent
  • Add Ginger garlic paste and fry for 2 mins on low heat
  • Add tumeric, red chilli and coriander powder and mix it well. Fry it on low heat for 2-3mins after adding a splash of water.
  • Now add the chicken and mix it well on a medium heat for 5-6minutes till the chicken is browned well, add salt and mix it well.
  • Add the cashew-red chilli-tomato paste, mix it well and fry for 5-6minutes on medium heat till oil separates and then add Garam masala powder, mix and cook for 1 minute
  • Add 200ml water and cover it to cook for 15 minutes, later add Kasuri Methi and mix it for 2 minutes, at this point Chicken Gravy is ready for meal.
